About Vazy Garden

This free app was created to help new gardeners manage the sowing of their seed collection.

You know the scenario...

  • It is the start of spring.
  • The sun is shining.
  • You decide to visit the garden centre.
  • You impulsively buy lots of pretty flower seeds.
  • You store them somewhere safe.
  • You rediscover them when it's too late!

With the Vazy Garden app you can manage the sowing of all those seeds in their correct months. You can also join a like-minded gardening community - see what others are adding to their plots. Registration is not required for viewing the site, but is for adding your plants.

Instructions

  •   Register your name and password.
  •   Log in to create a profile.
  •   Add your plants.
  •   Edit or delete them.
  •   Log the months to sow.
  •   Indicate if edible for humans
  •   Select if feed for a pet.
  •   Search for when seeds need to be sown.
  •   Get out there and start sowing those seeds!
